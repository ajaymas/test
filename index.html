About me

Welcome to my home page!

I am a Postdoctoral researcher in The College of Information science and Technology , Pennsylvania State University advised by JDr. Dinghao Wu. My research interests are in Cybersecurity, Virtualization and Machine learning, in particular for detection and analysis of malware and advanced persistenst threats.

Previously I was a postdoc in The Applied Research Ceneter, Florida International University, advised by Dr.Himanshu Upadhyay .

I received my PhD in computer science in 2017 from National Institute of Technology , Karnataka, Surathkal, India. where i advised by Dr. Jaidhar C.D. .
